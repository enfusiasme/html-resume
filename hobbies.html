Top 3 Hobbies of Mine

Reading

My passion for reading has been alive ever since I remeber picking up my first book. Ever since an early age, I've read book in genres ranging from classic literature to fiction to mythology. Currently I mainly read books on self-improvement and peotry, in order to continuously evolve and grow both mentally, emotionally, spiritually and in knowledge and wisdom. If i had to narrow down my favourite authors to two, I would have had to pick Yuvel Noah Harrari and Robin Sharma.

Drawing

I'm a strong believer of creativity, and the ability to express it in the most unique and authentic way imaginable. For me, my creative outlet is to draw. Unfortunately, I might not so much time to invest into a drawing nowadays, but whenever I do, the end result brings back the burning desire and admiration to life.

Wine Tastings

Ever since I started working as a Sommelier, I have been exposed to the outstanding world of wine, and I have been held captive in it ever since. My obsessive passion for exploring different terriors and trying wines from all around the world brings joy and happiness that some might call it crazy. Therefore, whenever I have a chance and a free day, I always try visit a winery or a wine shop. In my weird way, it is as if you are travelling the world from the comfort of a dark, cool cellar.
